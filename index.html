✦ Our Story

A Legacy of
Authentic Flavours

✦

Founded in 2004 by Shri Muthukrishnan, Hotel Gowri Kkrishna began as a single establishment in Pollachi and has since grown into a beloved chain of over 30 restaurants across Tamil Nadu — and even Bahrain.

Situated on New Scheme Road near the Gandhi Statue, our Pollachi flagship is the heart of the brand — a place where generations of families return for crispy dosas, comforting meals, and our celebrated filter coffee. Today, the second generation of the family continues to uphold every cherished recipe.

Filter Coffee

Our legendary decoction coffee — brewed the authentic Tamil way — is the reason regulars visit every single morning. The aroma is an experience on its own.
